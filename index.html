Favorites

How I met your mother

“How i met your mother” is about the life of a group of friends, told by one of them many years later to his teenage sons, oriented towards how he knew their mother.

The Office

"The Office" follows the day-to-day life of some eccentric workers at the Dunder Mifflin company, in a fresh and funny way, and with the appearance of a pseudo-documentary. Adapted by Grez Daniela ('The Simpsons') from the British series of the same name, 'The Office' parodies American culture through the experiences of its protagonists.

Attack on titan

"Attack on titan (Shingeki no kyogin)" is a remake of the Japanese manga that was written and drawn by Hajime Isayama and has been adapted into short films and television series.

Hulking, humanoid-looking, deformed creatures, called titans, threaten the world. Cities, to protect themselves, have built giant walls and trained soldiers. If the attack occurs, the swords and hooks of these squads will be ready to save the citizens.

Honorary Mention

Demon Slayer

"Demon Slayer" (Kimetsu no Yaiba) introduces Kamado Tanjiro, a young boy, quite intelligent and with a heart that doesn't fit in his chest. He lives with his family and earns money selling coal. But everything changes when his family is attacked and killed.

Kamado discovers that it was the fault of Oni, a rather supernatural and bloodthirsty demon. He and his sister Nezuko are the only survivors of the attack, but his sister was turned by the evil demon into one of his own kind.

The Boys

"The Boys" is the television adaptation of Garth Ennis' work that presents a superheroic world where the heroes are corrupted by fame. This blinkered, high-celebrity attitude leads them to endanger the world more than once.

A battle between ordinary people and superheroes ensues when the latter decide to unveil the truth about "The Seven" and an all-powerful multinational corporation that runs superheroes and hides all their dirty secrets, called Vought.Hughie Campbell is a simple tech store salesman who lives his day-to-day life with his partner, until a superhero of The Seven, named A-Tren, kills her by literally transfixing her with his super speed.
